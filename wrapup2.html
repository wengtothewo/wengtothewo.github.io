The Duo

Hugh (The Astronaut)

Hugh is the primary protagonist of Pragmata. He wears a highly advanced, heavy-duty EVA suit designed for the harsh lunar environment. What makes Hugh interesting is the level of detail on his gear—you can see every mechanical joint and sensor. In the gameplay, he acts as the protector, using his physical strength and high-tech weaponry to fend off robotic threats. His suit is a masterpiece of sci-fi design, blending realism with futuristic technology.

Diana (The Sidekick)

Diana is the mysterious young girl who accompanies Hugh. She is much more than a simple sidekick; she possesses incredible hacking powers that allow her to manipulate the digital world around them. As I mentioned before, her ability to weaken robotic enemies is a game-changer for the combat. Her design, with her long hair and blue jacket, provides a sharp contrast to Hugh’s metallic suit, making their partnership visually striking and unique.
