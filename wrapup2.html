The Characters

Hugh

Hugh is the playable main character of Pragmata. He wears a high tech, heavy duty EVA suit designed for the harsh lunar environment. What makes Hugh interesting is the level of detail on his gear—you can see every mechanical joint and sensor. In the gameplay, he acts as the protector, using his physical strength and high-tech weaponry to fend off robotic threats.

Diana

Diana is the mysterious young girl who travels with Hugh. She is much more than just a helper, she has amazing hacking powers that let her control the technological world around them. As I said before, her power to make robot enemies weaker is a huge help during a fight. Her look, with her long hair and blue jacket, is a big contrast to Hugh's metal suit.

Hostile Bots

The moon is filled with dangerous robotic enemies that hunt down Hugh and Diana. These bots come in many forms, from humanoids to massive mechanical beasts. They seem to be part of the lunar colony's defense system that has gone haywire. You can fight them using Hugh's weapons and Diana's hacking skills together to find their weak points.
