The Duo

Hugh

Hugh is the main character you play as in Pragmata. He wears a very high tech, heavy duty space suit built to survive on the moon. What makes Hugh look great is how detailed his suit is, you can see all the different parts and sensors on it. In the game, he is a protector who uses his strength and futuristic weapons to fight off robots. His suit is a perfect mix of real space gear and high tech imagination.

Diana

Diana is the mysterious young girl who travels with Hugh. She is much more than just a helper, she has amazing hacking powers that let her control the technological world around them. As I said before, her power to make robot enemies weaker is a huge help during a fight. Her look, with her long hair and blue jacket, is a big contrast to Hugh's metal suit. This makes them a very interesting team to play as.
