About the Development

Pragmata was first revealed in 2020. It is a brand new IP (Intellectual Property) from Capcom, designed to make use of the next generation hardware. The game is built using the RE (Reach for The Moon) Engine, which is the same technology used for the recent Resident Evil and Monster Hunter games.

The development team has focused heavily on creating a "profound sci fi setting" with realistic physics and lighting (using ray tracing). While the game was originally planned to release in 2022, the team decided to delay it to make sure that the final product meets the high expectations of fans.
