PRAGMATA

A New Vision of the Future

Pragmata is an upcoming sci-fi action-adventure game developed by Capcom. It was first revealed in 2020 and immediately caught my eye because of its unique aesthetic. The game features a lunar setting, a high-tech astronaut, and a mysterious young girl who seems to possess incredible powers. I chose this theme because the "Moon Dystopia" vibe is very visually interesting and allows for a great use of dark blues and metallic grays in web design.

The mystery of Pragmata lies in its environment. In the trailers, we see the Earth from the Moon's surface, but everything feels distorted and digital. It makes you wonder if the characters are in a simulation or a dying colony. My goal for this project is to showcase the art style of this game while practicing my HTML and CSS skills. This homepage acts as the gateway to the deeper secrets found on the other pages.

Design Roots

Our site's color profile is based on the game's official lighting.
