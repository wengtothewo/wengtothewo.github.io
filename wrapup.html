PRAGMATA

Pragmata is a game that will always be unique; it captures a sci-fi feeling that is honestly rare in modern gaming. Considering its Triple-A budget, the game has an incredible visual style. The graphics are highly detailed and immersive, especially when you look at Diana's hair or the mechanical parts of Hugh's high-tech suit.

The gameplay is also a major highlight for me. I love the concept of Diana acting as a sidekick who helps the main character along the way. Her hacking powers are a brilliant idea because they allow the enemy bots to be weakened, which lets Hugh deal more damage. Overall, I think Pragmata is an incredible video game that uses these unique ideas to make the sci-fi genre feel fresh and different.
