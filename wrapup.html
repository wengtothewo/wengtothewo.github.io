PRAGMATA

Pragmata is a game that will always be unique, it captures the sci-fi feeling that is just rare in modern gaming. Considering its triple A budget, the game has a unique visual style and quality, the graphics are incredibly detailed and immersive like Diana's hair and Hugh's high tech suit. The gameplay is also a key part of the game, I really like the concept of Diana being the little girl who helps the main character, almost like a sidekick. He hacking powers are a good idea as they allow the bots to be weaker, and can allow Hugh to deal more damage. In general I think that Pragmata is a incredible triple A video game, using unique ideas to make a sci-fi game different.
