Cars 3 Movie Profile

Cars 3 follows Lightning McQueen as he faces a new generation of blazing-fast racers. To get back in the game, he will need the help of an eager young race technician, Cruz Ramirez, and inspiration from the late Fabulous Hudson Hornet.

Fast Facts

  • Release Date: June 16, 2017
  • Director: Brian Fee
  • Music by: Randy Newman
  • Box Office: $383.9 Million
