The World

The Lunar Frontier

Pragmata takes place in a future version of the Moon. The lunar surface is a desolate and eerie environment, having earth in the background really shows how Hugh and Diana are from home. The Lunar surface has a mix of high tech facilities and natural rocky terrain of the Moon.

Digital Distortion

The most interesting part of the world is how reality seems to break. In the trailer, we see the sky shatter into glass like shapes and digital glitches appearing in the world. This creates a mystery. Is this the real moon, or is it a simulation? Playing the game with these glitches is a major part of the game's visual style and atmosphere.
